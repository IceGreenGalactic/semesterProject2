How to Ship

For Buyers

Congratulations on winning the auction! Here's what you need to know about shipping:

  1. Shipping Included: Shipping costs are always included in the item's price, so there's no need to worry about additional fees.
  2. Contact Seller: Reach out to the seller to confirm shipping details and provide any special instructions.
  3. Tracking Information: Once the item is shipped, the seller will provide tracking information so you can monitor its progress.
  4. Receive Item: Upon delivery, inspect the item to ensure it matches the description and meets your expectations.

For Sellers

Here's what sellers need to know about shipping items to buyers:

  1. Shipping Details: Ensure that you have accurate shipping information for the buyer, including their address and any specific instructions they may have provided.
  2. Packaging: Pack the item securely to prevent damage during transit. Use appropriate packaging materials and consider adding extra padding for fragile items.
  3. Shipping Process: Choose a reliable shipping method and provide the buyer with tracking information once the item is dispatched.

By following these steps, both buyers and sellers can ensure a smooth and successful shipping experience. If you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to reach out to AuctionHouse support.
