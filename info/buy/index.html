How to Buy

Purchasing items on AuctionHouse is simple and accessible to registered users. Follow these steps to place a bid and acquire your desired items:

  1. Browse Listings: Explore the available listings to find items that catch your interest.
  2. Place Bid: Once you've found an item you wish to bid on, enter your bid amount and submit it. Remember, only registered users can place bids.
  3. Monitor Bids: Keep an eye on the bidding activity to stay informed about the status of your bids.
  4. Win Auction: If your bid is the highest when the auction ends, you'll secure the item.
  5. Complete Transaction: Follow the provided instructions to finalize the transaction and receive your item.

As a registered user, you'll receive 1000 credits upon joining AuctionHouse, which you can use for bidding. Additional credits can be earned by selling items on the platform. Enjoy the thrill of bidding and discovering unique treasures on AuctionHouse!
