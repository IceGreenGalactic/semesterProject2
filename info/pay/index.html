How to Pay

At AuctionHouse, payment for items is made using credits. Here's how you can pay for your purchases on our platform:

  1. Available Credits: Before making a purchase, ensure that you have sufficient credits available in your account.
  2. Place Bid: When placing a bid on an item, the bid amount will be deducted from your available credits.
  3. Successful Bid: If your bid is the winning bid when the auction ends, the purchase amount will be deducted from your credits, and you'll secure the item.
  4. Insufficient Credits: If you do not have enough credits to place a bid, you may need to earn more credits by selling items on AuctionHouse.

By using credits as the primary form of payment, AuctionHouse ensures a seamless and convenient shopping experience for all users. Start bidding and enjoy the thrill of acquiring unique items today!
